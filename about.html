The Beginning

Born from darkness,
into the light

My path into spiritual guidance did not begin in a classroom or a course — it began in my own unravelling. At 24, I found myself at a crossroads so overwhelming that logic alone could not lead me out. It was in that sacred stillness — a friend's tarot deck, a quiet room, a question I could barely speak — that the first thread of clarity appeared.

"I did not choose this work. It chose me — and when it did, everything changed."

What followed was a decade of deep study: tarot symbolism under a Jungian psychologist, Rune reading with a keeper in Jaipur, and Reiki energy healing through three years of devoted practice. Each modality revealed a new language for the same ancient truth — that every soul already holds its own answers.

Infinite Soul Awakening was born from a personal calling — not as a brand, but as a promise: that every person who came to me would leave with more clarity than they arrived with, regardless of what the cards showed.

The Practice

Where tradition meets
intuition

Every session I offer draws from three distinct traditions — each one a gift passed down through unbroken lineages of wisdom keepers. Tarot illuminates the narrative arc of your life. Energy healing clears the static so your own voice can finally be heard.

My approach is never prescriptive. I am not here to tell you what to do — I am here to help you hear what you already know. The clearer your inner signal, the more decisively you can move through the world.

Sessions are held in strict confidence, and I work with a deeply diverse community of clients — entrepreneurs and artists, mothers and seekers, sceptics and believers. What unites them is simple: they want truth, not comfort. And I am here to provide it.
