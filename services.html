Common Questions

Things you may
want to know

How do I choose the right session?

You may choose a session based on the area in which you are seeking guidance, clarity, mindfulness support, or personal reflection. Session descriptions are provided to help you select the most suitable option.

Are services available internationally?

Yes. All services are offered online and are accessible to clients in different locations worldwide.

Do I need prior experience with wellness practices?

No. Sessions are designed to be accessible to individuals from all backgrounds and experience levels. No prior knowledge is required.

Are sessions private?

Information voluntarily shared during the booking and session process is handled respectfully and privately in accordance with applicable privacy practices and the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

Do I need to share photographs or personal images?

No. Photographs, videos, identification documents, or personal images are not required for any service.

How are sessions conducted?

Sessions and guidance are provided online through chat, voice notes, scheduled calls, written reflections, or other agreed digital communication methods depending on the selected service. All sessions are online only. In-person and video call sessions are not provided.

Do I need to perform any practices myself?

No. Services are practitioner-led, and the selected session or wellness-focused practice is conducted independently by the practitioner.

Are results guaranteed or intended as professional advice?

No. Services are intended for personal wellness, mindfulness, self-reflection, and spiritual exploration purposes only. Individual experiences and outcomes may vary. Services are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or replace medical, psychological, legal, or financial advice, treatment, or professional services.
