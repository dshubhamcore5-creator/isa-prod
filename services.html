Common Questions

Things you may
want to know

Do I need to believe in tarot for it to work?

Not at all. Sceptics often receive the most powerful readings precisely because they are open — not filtering the insights through expectation. Curiosity is the only requirement.

Are sessions available outside of India?

Yes. All services are available online via video call, which means we can work together regardless of your location. Clients currently span 18+ countries across six continents.

How should I prepare for my session?

Come with one or two specific questions or areas of focus. Find a quiet, private space. Hydrate well beforehand. If you have a crystal or meaningful object, feel free to hold it during the session.

Is everything I share kept confidential?

Absolutely. Your session content, personal details, and anything shared in the questionnaire are held in complete confidence and are never shared with any third party under any circumstance.
